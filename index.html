Visit Toronto

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Toronto.

Top three activities to do at Toronto

Go to the top of the CN tower

The CN Tower is a 553.3 m-high concrete communications and observation tower in downtown Toronto

Watch Raptors game at the Scotiabank Arena

Scotiabank Arena, formerly known as Air Canada Centre, is a multi-purposed arena located on Bay Street in the South Core district of Downtown Toronto

Shop at the heart of downtown Toronto

Downtown Toronto is a buzzing area filled with skyscrapers, restaurants, nightlife, and an eclectic mix of neighbourhoods
